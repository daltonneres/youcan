About You Can

You Can is an English school with a gamified methodology that turns learning into a fun and effective experience. We offer both in-person and online classes for children, teens, and adults.

Our History, Mission & Values

History

You Can was born with the purpose of transforming English learning into something light, fun, and motivating. We combine technology, gamification, and a humanized approach to spark students’ curiosity and joy in learning.

Mission

To provide innovative and effective learning experiences, helping our students achieve fluency through fun and consistent practice.

Values

  • Learning with fun and purpose
  • Respect and empathy for each student
  • Constant innovation in our methodology
  • Commitment to real results

Purpose

To transform lives through global communication, showing that everyone can — and should — believe in their potential to learn English!

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the classes in-person or online?

We offer both! Students can choose the format that best fits their schedule.

What is gamification?

It’s the use of game elements to make learning more interactive, fun, and effective.

What age groups can participate?

We offer courses for children (3–10), teens (11–15), and adults (15+).
